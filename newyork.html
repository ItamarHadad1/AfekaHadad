New York



New York is one of the most famous and dynamic cities in the world. Known as "The Big Apple", it is filled with skyscrapers, museums, parks, and iconic landmarks visited by millions every year.

One of the most well-known places in New York is Times Square, famous for its bright lights, giant screens, theaters, and constant activity. It is one of the busiest and most recognizable locations in the entire world.
