Paris



Paris is one of the most beautiful and famous cities in the world. It is known for its art, food, architecture, culture, and romantic streets. Millions of tourists visit Paris every year to enjoy its elegant boulevards, museums, cafes, and historical sites.

One of the most iconic landmarks in Paris is the Eiffel Tower. Built in 1889, it has become a global symbol of France. Visitors can go up to the top of the tower for an amazing view of the entire city.
